# Game Rules

## Goal

Graduate Together is fully cooperative. The table wins only when every player graduates.

## Winning conditions

The team wins when all of the following are true:

- the shared credit target is reached
- every player has completed `2` laps around the board
- every player has completed Goal, Support, and SMART milestones

## Turn flow

1. Amber announces whose turn it is.
2. The player rolls a six-sided die.
3. Their piece moves around the square board.
4. The landing tile triggers either a symbol card, a mini-challenge, or a special tile effect.
5. The turn resolves and the next player begins.

## Core progress systems

- `Credits` are shared by the whole group and move the team toward graduation.
- `Support tokens` are shared resources used to help players who are behind.
- `Milestones` are personal progress checks:
  - `Goal`
  - `Support`
  - `SMART`

## Team Assist

Team Assist lets the table spend `1` support token to help one player progress. It can be used once per turn and advances that player's next unfinished milestone. It is meant to keep the group moving together instead of leaving one player behind.

## Special tiles

- `Mentor Moment`
- `Reflection Bench`
- `Slow Down Zone`
- `SMART Check-in`

## Game lengths

- `Quick Game`: 24-tile board, lower shared credit target
- `Full Journey`: 32-tile board, higher shared credit target

The shared credit target scales with player count.

## Table tone

- Keep prompts supportive and easy to follow.
- Make progress visible.
- Celebrate growth.
- Have fun and graduate together.
