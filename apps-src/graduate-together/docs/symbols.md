# Symbols

Graduate Together uses these eight tutoring symbols exactly as written below.

## 1. Star - Your Hopes, Wishes, Dreams and Desires!

- Prompt: What is the goal of this session?
- Use in game: personal goals, direction, and milestone focus

## 2. Circles - Teamwork makes the Dream Work!

- Prompt: Who can help them reach their goals in today's session?
- Use in game: support actions, shared help, catch-up mechanics

## 3. Clapperboard - Take 1 to Take 101

- Prompt: What mistakes and ACTions are we going through with the Learner to help the Learner figure out what they do not know?
- Use in game: retries, learning from mistakes, challenge cards

## 4. Litmus Strip - What are Your Passions?

- Prompt: Which passions are you bringing to help them understand the work and make it relevant? Or what can you use as a reward once they get through this?
- Use in game: motivation, relevance, rewards

## 5. Heart - You Can Do It!

- Prompt: Are any stories preventing them from reaching their stars? Are you helping them create new stories of confidence and ability?
- Use in game: confidence, recovery, reflection

## 6. Amber Highlighted Stop Sign - Don't Rush Through Life!

- Prompt: Are they able to stop freezing? Are you able to slow it down for them?
- Use in game: pacing help, slow-down options, reset moments

## 7. Target - S.M.A.R.T. Goal Setting System

- Prompt: Are they closer to reaching their stars than the last tutoring session? What do they need to do between tutoring sessions to get closer to their stars?
- Use in game: progress checks, planning, milestone review

## 8. Happy Face - Happy...Just Because!

- Prompt: How are you helping them keep their spirits up while they strive for their GOAL? Are you helping them control their own feelings?
- Use in game: morale boosts, celebrations, recovery
