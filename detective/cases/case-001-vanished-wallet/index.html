Case 001: The Vanished Wallet

This report documents the disappearance of a student wallet during scheduled Physical Education class.

Date: Tuesday
Location: Locker Row C (Gym Wing)
Reporting Office: Administration
Status: Under Review

Incident Summary

At approximately 1:35 pm, student Riley Chen reported that their wallet was missing from locker C-14 following Gym class.

The locker showed no signs of forced entry. The wallet had been placed inside shortly before class began.

Administration collected statements and records. All materials are provided for review.

Investigation Goal

Determine:

  • Who accessed the locker
  • How access was gained
  • Which evidence confirms responsibility
